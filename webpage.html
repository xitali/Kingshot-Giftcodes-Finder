How to Redeem Kingshot Gift Codes

For Android Users:

  1. Complete the in-game tutorial.
  2. Tap your profile icon (top-left).
  3. Open Settings > Gift Code.
  4. Enter the code exactly (case-sensitive!).
  5. Tap Redeem.

For iOS Users:

  1. Find your Player ID in your profile.
  2. Visit the official redemption portal: ks-giftcode.centurygame.com.
  3. Log in with your Player ID.
  4. Input the code and click Exchange.
